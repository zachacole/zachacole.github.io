    • Together with an incredible team of designers, researchers, and producers, I designed a suite of features to help Lyft drivers make the most of their time on the road.

    Driving smarter

    Lyft drivers are working towards unique life goals â€” bootstrapping a business, paying for school, or buying a home. We designed tools to help drivers better understand when and where to drive in order to make the most of their time on the road, and hit their goals sooner.

    Increasing driver activations

    Getting started with Lyft can be a challenge for drivers who are unsure of what to expect. I designed a framework for bite-sized educational lessons for newly approved Lyft drivers to put them at ease before their first ride.

    Events for drivers

    In many cities, events give drivers high-demand, valuable rides. We designed a tool to allow drivers to better plan their week based on the upcoming event calendar.
